Rock, Paper, Scissors Game

Rock paper scissors is a game that can be played with 2 or more players.
In this version, one player will compete against the computer.

Rules:
Player 1 will choose 1 of 3 options: Rock, paper, or scissors.
The computer will randomly select one as well.
To win the game, player 1 must correctly choose the object that will beat the computer - luck of the draw!

Rock beats scissors.
Scissors beats paper.
Paper beats rock.

Once the player enters their selection
A message will appear to notify the player is they won or loss against the computer.

The player can then choose to play again or quit.
